About Me

With over a decade of experience behind the lens, I specialize in creating images that are not just seen, but felt. My approach combines technical precision with artistic intuition to capture authentic moments and emotions.

After studying Fine Arts at the Rhode Island School of Design, I began my career as a photojournalist before transitioning to commercial and artistic photography. My work has been featured in National Geographic, Vogue, and numerous gallery exhibitions worldwide.

When I'm not shooting for clients, you can find me teaching photography workshops or working on personal projects that explore the intersection of light, shadow, and human emotion.
